Box Maker

Create a box for your model!

Upload an STL, and the box will automatically be sized to fit your STL's dimensions. You can also manually specify the width, height, and depth of your desired box to output an SVG template you can cut and fold to create a package.

The SVG will contain cut lines (solid) and score lines (dashed).
